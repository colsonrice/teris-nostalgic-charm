A Note

Dear friend,

What you're looking at is, in a way, a record of small attentions — the little teddy bear someone wears because their grandmother had one just like it; the lawyer's brooch worn the day she was sworn in; the figure skater's earrings tucked into a Christmas card.

I've spent years quietly gathering these pieces — vintage and vintage-inspired charm jewelry in gold plate, sterling silver, antiqued brass, and pewter. This page is a small front porch I built so they could be met properly. Reach out about anything that catches your eye.

Thank you for stopping in.
— Teri
